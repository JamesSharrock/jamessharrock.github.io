Hey, I'm James Sharrock. I'm currently studying BSc Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Lancaster. This is a website portfolio to showcase the work I'm currently doing and to act as a portfolio of my projects.

I began programming in HTML and CSS where I designed my website from the ground up, this site was created to work on all devices of all screen sizes. From there I learnt Python and the ideas behind programming: data structures, selection and iteration. I used python for my GCSE Programming project in creating an ASCII dice game that could be played between two players. At A-Level I continued with python and used Unity a cross-platform game engine to design a racing game for mobile using C++ scripts. At University in the first term we began with C a language that is one of the most widely used and is completely flexible when it comes to reading and wrting from memory. In the second term we learnt Java a language similar to C except that it introduced the idea of Object-Oriented programming using classes and methods. Using Java I undertook two projects: Colourdle and Klask.

I have work experience at one of the UK's largest engineering solutions companies where I shadowed engineers, using AutoCAD software to perform revisions of blueprints for a section of a new smart motorway. I then further volunteered with the company in the summer of 2019, maintaining a good relationship. I was in the Valish Young Enterprise Team of 2020 where we came 2nd in the North West. As a team we developed eco-friendly solutions to car air fresheners reducing waste through a reusable wooden template and allowing for personalised scents. I also entered the UK Youth Rocketry Competition with a team where we had plans to uphold the title held by our predecessors who won the competition in 2019. Unfortunately due to Covid-19 this was cancelled.

In my spare time I enjoy kayaking, cycling, walking and going to the gym. I have a blue belt in taekwondo and my proficiency in swimming as well as a life-saving skills qualification. Being active and maintaining a good diet is important to me and I enjoy cooking. I am passionate about Formula One and support the Mclaren constructors team.
